PURPOSE OF EDUCATION

“THE PURPOSE OF EDUCATION IS TO PREPARE THE YOUNG TO EDUCATE THEMSELVES THROUGHOUT THEIR LIVES”

Preschool education is not just about art, music, dance, fun, and fantasy, it is to provide comprehensive learning objectives and having a structured way to achieve these objectives.

The 4 major components to achieve these objectives are:
  • The philosophy.
  • The curriculum and methodology.
  • The learning tools are offered to the child.
  • The teachers who facilitate learning.

Our curriculum balances these 4 components. The core philosophy is inspired by the Montessori method. Each material has been designed following this philosophy. The teachers have been trained effectively on the philosophy, curriculum, and materials. Academically equipped and socially adept children are what we are striving to provide this world.

FAQ

Academic Excellence and Best Curriculum in Chennai.

At Rising Star School of Excellence, we offer an exceptional academic curriculum that ensures students are well-prepared for higher education and life beyond school. Our curriculum is designed to develop critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a strong academic foundation in subjects like Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies, making us one of the top schools in Chennai.

Our team of dedicated teachers and staff play a significant role in the success of our students. At Rising Star School of Excellence, we believe that effective learning is supported by compassionate educators who understand each child’s needs and potential. Our teachers inspire and motivate students to do their best, and our small student-to-teacher ratio ensures personalized attention to every learner.

We provide world-class facilities, including modern classrooms, smart boards, well-equipped science labs, a library, and sports facilities. These resources enable our students to experience the best of both academics and extracurricular activities. Our school infrastructure is designed to support a comprehensive, engaging, and enjoyable learning experience for all our students.

Extracurricular activities are an integral part of life at Rising Star. We offer a wide range of programs such as sports, arts, music, dance, and cultural activities that help students develop their talents and become well-rounded individuals. Our holistic approach ensures that every child discovers their unique abilities and interests, making us one of the most sought-after schools in Chennai for extracurriculars.

In addition to academic subjects, we prioritize the emotional and social development of our students. Through Social-Emotional Learning (SEL) programs, we help students build resilience, self-awareness, and empathy—skills that are essential in today’s world. Our SEL-focused curriculum ensures that students become not just academically competent but also emotionally intelligent.

We incorporate technology in education to enhance the learning experience. Our students are exposed to digital tools, interactive lessons, and online learning platforms that make learning engaging and relevant in today’s digital age. By using the latest educational technologies, Rising Star School of Excellence helps students stay ahead in an ever-evolving world.

At Rising Star School of Excellence, we believe that an excellent curriculum is essential for the holistic development of a child. Our carefully crafted curriculum balances academic rigor with creativity, offering a range of subjects that provide students with opportunities to explore and excel.
