About Us

Rising Stars School Of Excellence

Rising Stars School of Excellence is one of the schools under Sheela Educational and charitable Trust. Our main motto is to give quality education for all the children without any prejudice. We give an environment where everyone is respected and valued .We highly believe in a strong bond between parents, teachers and management to create a good future for the child with values of excellence, respect, creativity, kindness, confidence, compassion, and capable to making a positive impact on the world.We have skilled teaching and non-teaching staff, who are dedicated and committed to the care and learning of our students. In Rising Stars ‘STARS’ represent Self Reliant, Tenacious, Ameliorative, Radiant, Scholar.

Our children represent our hopes and dreams for the future,and they deserve the best education that, we can provide them. We believe every student has the potential to succeed in different fields, and we are dedicated to making a difference in children's lives by encouraging them to open their minds to learn, discover and achieve. Our basic principles are respect,kindness and responsibility to govern teaching and learning in a peaceful, productive and orderly learning environment that meets the academic ,social and developmental needs of all our students. We respect our students likes and dislikes ,identify their talents and keep an ear open for the students perspective.

OUR VISION

To enrich and inspire children to reach their fullest potential as kind, independent, confident and curious individuals,

OUR MISSION

A productive environment ,providing great knowledge and social, cultural emotional development to the students

WHAT DIFFERENTIATES US?

  • Child centric approach rather than teacher-centric
  • Strong Montessori-based philosophy combined with best practices of early childhood methodology.
  • Goes way beyond song & dance routines, nursery rhymes, and simple playing with toys
  • Materials and tools for learning designed for the curriculum
  • A methodology that instill a sense of purpose & interest to work hard in the child
  • Revolutionary assessment technique
  • A structured learning program for the overall development of the child
